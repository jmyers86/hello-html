Jean Giraud "Moebius"

Born in France in 1938, Moebius became one of the most influential sci-fi artists of all time. He made a name for himself in Europe with his western comic "Blueberry" and later founded the magazine "Metal Hurlant" (Heavy Metal) which became massive in the U.S. He has done concept work for major films like Tron, The Fifth Element, Alien and more.
